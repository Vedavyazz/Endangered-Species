Endangered Species

Animals that are at risk of extinction and need protection.

List of Endangered Animals

  1. Bengal Tiger
  2. Mountain Gorilla
  3. African Elephant
  4. Giant Panda
  5. Blue Whale
  6. Hawksbill Turtle

1. Bengal Tiger

The Bengal tiger is endangered due to habitat loss and illegal hunting. Conservation programs help protect this species.

2. Mountain Gorilla

Mountain gorillas live in African forests and are threatened by disease and habitat destruction.

3. African Elephant

African elephants are endangered mainly because of ivory poaching and loss of habitat.

4. Giant Panda

Giant pandas depend on bamboo forests which are decreasing due to human activities.

5. Blue Whale

The blue whale is the largest animal on Earth and faces threats from pollution and ship collisions.

6. Hawksbill Turtle

This sea turtle is endangered because of illegal shell trade and ocean pollution.
